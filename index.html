About Emarath Global

Emarath Global Private Limited is the India-based arm of Emarath UAE, operating from HiLITE Business Park, Calicut (Kozhikode), Kerala. The company functions as a strategic hub for technology, operations, sourcing, and regional expansion, supporting Emarath’s growing presence across the Middle East and international markets.

Aligned with Emarath UAE

The Calicut team works closely with global stakeholders to ensure operational efficiency, quality control, and scalable cross-border commerce solutions for premium product categories.
