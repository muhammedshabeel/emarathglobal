FAQ

Quick answers for UAE-based clients and partners.

What timelines should we expect for a new engagement?
Most discovery-to-plan phases take 1–2 weeks. Execution timelines depend on scope, vendors, and compliance gates.
Do you support compliance and documentation workflows?
Yes — we build lightweight documentation, audit trails, QA checklists, and vendor compliance routines.
How do you handle payment terms and commercial structure?
We align on milestones, deliverables, and reporting. Terms can be structured monthly or phase-based, depending on the work.
Can you work with existing vendors and internal teams?
That’s the default. We integrate with your stakeholders and vendors using clear ownership, SLAs, and escalation paths.
Which industries do you support?
Corporate, property, logistics, retail, SMEs — especially UAE-linked operations and premium commerce categories.
How do you ensure quality for premium categories?
Defined standards, sampling routines, QA checkpoints, vendor audits, and continuous feedback loops.
