FAQ

Quick answers for UAE-based clients and partners.

What timelines should we expect for a new engagement?

Most engagements start with a 1–2 week discovery + planning phase. Execution timelines depend on scope and stakeholders.

Do you support compliance and documentation workflows?

Yes. We align documentation, QA processes, vendor checks, and operational records to meet regional expectations.

How do you handle payment terms and commercial structure?

We support milestone-based billing or monthly retainers. Terms depend on engagement type and operational ownership.

Can you work with existing vendors and internal teams?

That’s the normal setup. We integrate into your ecosystem — reporting, SLAs, and escalation paths included.

Which industries do you support?

Corporate clients, property, logistics, retail, SMEs — with special strength in premium e-commerce categories.

How do you ensure quality for premium categories?

Sampling rules, vendor audits, QC checkpoints, and data-backed feedback loops. Premium demands consistency — we enforce it.
